Best Phones for Students Under NPR 25,000

By Aayushman Thakulla

Looking for a good phone on a student budget? Here's what to look for under NPR 25,000:

  • Good battery life (at least 4000mAh)
  • Decent camera for online classes and notes
  • At least 4GB RAM for smooth multitasking
  • Brands like Xiaomi, Realme, and Samsung offer great value

Always compare specs and read reviews before buying. A good phone is a great tool for learning and staying connected!
