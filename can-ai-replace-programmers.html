Can AI Replace Programmers? My Thoughts

By Aayushman Thakulla

AI is getting smarter, but can it really replace programmers? In my opinion, AI is a powerful tool that helps programmers, not replaces them. AI can write code, suggest solutions, and automate repetitive tasks, but it still needs human creativity, problem-solving, and understanding of real-world needs.

Programmers who learn to use AI will be more productive and in demand. The future is about collaboration between humans and AI, not competition. So, keep learning and use AI as your coding partner!
