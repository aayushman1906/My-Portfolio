Top 5 AI Tools That Made My Work Easier

By Aayushman Thakulla

AI tools can save you time and boost your creativity. Here are my top 5 favorites:

  • ChatGPT – For writing, coding help, and brainstorming ideas.
  • Canva AI – For quick, beautiful designs and graphics.
  • Grammarly – For checking grammar and improving writing.
  • Google Lens – For visual search and translating text in images.
  • Notion AI – For organizing notes and boosting productivity.

Try these tools to make your work and study life easier and more fun!
