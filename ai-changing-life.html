How Artificial Intelligence is Changing Our Daily Life

By Aayushman Thakulla

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is everywhere! From the moment you wake up and check your phone, AI is working behind the scenes. It powers your camera's face recognition, suggests videos on YouTube, and even helps you navigate traffic with Google Maps.

AI is also making healthcare smarter, helping doctors diagnose diseases faster. In education, AI tools help students learn better and teachers personalize lessons. The future will be even more AI-powered, making our lives easier, faster, and more connected!
