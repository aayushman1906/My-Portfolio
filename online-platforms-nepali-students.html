Top Online Platforms for Nepali Students to Learn Coding

By Aayushman Thakulla

There are many great online platforms for Nepali students to learn coding. Here are some of my favorites:

  • freeCodeCamp – Free, interactive coding lessons and projects.
  • Coursera – University-level courses, some in Nepali language.
  • YouTube – Channels like mine, plus many others for every topic.
  • Nepali tech blogs – Local content and community support.
  • Facebook groups & Discord servers – Connect with other learners and get help.

Start with one platform, practice regularly, and join online communities to stay motivated!
